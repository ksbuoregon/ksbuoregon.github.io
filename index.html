Biography

My most recent employment is running my own law firm as a general family law practitioner in the beautiful town of Sandy, Oregon. My skill set includes the ability to manage over eighty team members to completea job or complete a project on my own, as appropriate. I have managed payroll, prepared tax documents and filings, prepared grant applications, and general business adminstration in addition to the paperwork required for each of my clients. In my worklife, I have worked at a US Embassy, managed two non-profits, and been a consultant for board of directors in the United States and Australia. As a child, I lived on every continent except for Antartica. I still love to travel, although Oregon has been home for my adult life.
